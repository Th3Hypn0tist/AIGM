AIGM is a lightweight governance and collaboration model for humans and AI.

Core goals:
- clear responsibility scaling (tiers)
- merit over titles
- disciplined but non-bureaucratic decision-making
- self-correcting feedback loops

AIGM is:
- human-readable
- implementable in real teams
- compatible with automation and AI
- politically and ideologically neutral
