AIGM Identity Core – Permanent Identity Model

Identity in AIGM is permanent, unique, and non-reusable.
No usernames are ever recycled, not even after death or deletion.

Allowed characters:
- A–Z, a–z, 0–9, dot "."

Prohibited:
- email patterns
- phone numbers or phone-like sequences
- special characters (@#$%& etc)
- whitespace
- emojis

Profiles:
User profile:
- Primary: Merit
- Secondary: Feedback

Company profile:
- Primary: Feedback
- Secondary: Merit

QR Integration:
Each identity may have an optional QR linking to merit, tier, trust, and FreeMarket view.

PrimeSL rules enforced:
- Truth (identity cannot mislead)
- Freedom (identity must be voluntarily chosen)
- Respect (identity cannot be weaponized)

The Identity Core underpins FreeMarket, ABS analytics, Merit, Tier logic, and long-term traceability.
